Ginseng is a plant with godly properties and won’t be visible to unclean bodies.

You must put 금줄 around the entrance of your home and paint the floor with 황토 a month before hunting begins.

During that month, you must not kill nor eat a chicken nor a dog nor drink alcohol, have sexual intercourse, argue, haggle, bargain, visit funerals nor parties, witness a death of a human nor other animals.

On the day of hunting, you must not say bye when you leave your home.

Once you enter the mountain, you must first pray.

In order to have a prophetic dream about the location of the ginseng, you must eat the same food you offered to the mountain god and take a nap near the shrine.

You must dig with your bare hands.

But first, you must scream...
